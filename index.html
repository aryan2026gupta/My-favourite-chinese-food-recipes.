Chinese Soup Dumpling

This is going to be my favourite recipe.

List of ingredients

Dough for wrapper 200 grams
Meat for filling 150 grams
Vegetables for filling 150 grams

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Make the aspic (meat gelatin).
  2. Step 2: Make the dough for the dumpling wrappers.
  3. Step 3: Make the filling.
  4. Step 4: Wrap the dumpling wrapper around a small portion of filling.
  5. Step 5: Steam and enjoy!

Chinese Vegetable Dumpling

This is going to be my second favourite recipe.

List of ingredients

Dough for wrapper 200 grams
Vegetables for filling 150 grams
Noodles for filling 150 grams

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Chop the vegetables for filling.
  2. Step 2: Make the dough for the dumpling wrappers.
  3. Step 3: Combine small portions of noodle with the vegetable filling.
  4. Step 4: Wrap the dumpling wrapper around a small portion of filling.
  5. Step 5: Steam and enjoy!

Chinese Fried Dumpling

This is going to be my third favourite recipe.

List of ingredients

Dough for wrapper 200 grams
Pork meat 150 grams
Chives 50 grams

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Season your pork with spices for filling (garlic and chili powder).
  2. Step 2: Make the dough for the dumpling wrappers.
  3. Step 3: Combine small portions of chives with the pork filling.
  4. Step 4: Wrap the dumpling wrapper around a small portion of filling.
  5. Step 5: Steam and enjoy!
  6. Gupta's Tip: After cooking your dumpling, soak refrigerate them in dumpling sauce for 8 hours and microwave.
